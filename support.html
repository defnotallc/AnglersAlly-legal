Frequently Asked Questions

I purchased Remove Ads but I still see ads. What do I do?

Open Settings → Remove Ads → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID region and the approximate purchase date and we'll help.

How do I get a refund for Remove Ads?

Refunds are handled directly by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, find Angler's Ally in your purchase history, and request a refund.

The app shows the wrong fish/photo for a species. How do I report it?

Email us with the species name and the issue. If you can attach a screenshot from the App, even better. We'll fix it in the next release.

Will fishing regulations in the App match my state?

Regulatory notes inside the App are general references only and should not be relied upon for legal compliance. Always check your state's official wildlife agency for current licenses, seasons, slot/bag limits, and gear restrictions before fishing.

The App is crashing or behaving strangely.

First, try fully closing the App (swipe up from the App Switcher) and reopening it. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what you were doing when it happened.

How do I disable ads without paying?

The free version of Angler's Ally is supported by advertising. The only ad-free option is the one-time Remove Ads in-app purchase. We don't use subscriptions.

Does the App work offline?

Yes. All reference data (species, gear, knots) is bundled with the App. You only need an internet connection to view advertising, complete an in-app purchase, or restore a previous purchase.

How do I revoke location or tracking permission?

Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security:

  • Location Services → Angler's Ally — set to "Never" to revoke location access.
  • Tracking — toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track" or revisit your choice for Angler's Ally individually.
