Privacy Policy — Angler's Ally

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Angler's Ally ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our iOS application. Angler's Ally is published by DefNotA LLC and is distributed through the Apple App Store under bundle identifier DefNotA.AnglerAlly.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Angler's Ally does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number) from you directly.

Location (Optional)

If you grant location permission, the App may use your device's approximate location solely to rank or filter in-app reference content (such as species commonly found near you). Your location is used on-device only and is never transmitted to our servers or stored remotely. You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

Local Data

The App stores the following on your device only, using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Your onboarding completion state
  • Your "Remove Ads" purchase flag
  • Display and ad-consent preferences

This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Angler's Ally displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Identifier for Advertisers, if you permit tracking)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • App usage data relevant to ad delivery

Google's use of information is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can learn about opting out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings.

Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

The App uses Google's UMP SDK to collect any advertising consent required by your region (e.g., GDPR in the EU/UK). UMP's data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit

The optional one-time "Remove Ads" purchase is processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never receive your payment card or Apple ID credentials. Receipts are stored on-device and on Apple's servers. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

If prompted, you may choose to allow or deny tracking by advertising networks. Choosing "Ask App Not to Track" limits ads to non-personalized delivery but does not eliminate ads. Your choice is respected regardless of the option selected.

In-App Purchases

Angler's Ally is free with an optional one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase titled "Remove Ads" (Product ID DefNotA.AnglerAlly.removeAds). The purchase suppresses all banner and interstitial ads. Refund requests are governed by Apple's policies — please use reportaproblem.apple.com.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain, export, or delete on our servers. Local data on your device can be cleared at any time by deleting the App.

Children's Privacy

Angler's Ally is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information in some way, please contact us and we will promptly address the issue.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA / CPRA), or similar U.S. state laws. These may include the right to access, correct, or delete information about you, and the right to opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise any right, email the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
