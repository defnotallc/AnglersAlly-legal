# Privacy Policy — Angler's Ally

**Effective date:** May 20, 2026
**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

DefNotA LLC ("**we**", "**us**", or "**our**") operates the **Angler's
Ally** mobile application (the "**App**") for iOS, distributed through
the Apple App Store under bundle identifier `DefNotA.AnglerAlly`.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how that
information is used, and the choices you have. By downloading or using
the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Summary

- The App does **not** require an account or login.
- The App does **not** collect your name, email, phone number, address,
  contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or health data.
- The App **may** request your **approximate location** if you opt into
  a location-based feature (e.g., showing species commonly found near
  you). Location is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our
  servers.
- The App displays **advertising** supplied by Google AdMob and uses
  Google's **User Messaging Platform (UMP)** to obtain any required
  advertising consent. AdMob may collect a device-level Advertising
  Identifier (IDFA) for ad personalization and measurement, subject to
  Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.
- The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("**Remove Ads**")
  processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment
  card or Apple ID credentials.

If you would like to remove advertising entirely, purchase the **Remove
Ads** upgrade from within the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

The App does not have user accounts and does not ask you to enter
personally identifying information. You may optionally share your
device location with the App (see Section 2.3).

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the App, the following limited information may be
collected automatically:

| Category | Examples | Purpose | Stored on our servers? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Advertising Identifier (IDFA)** | Apple-issued anonymous device ID | Ad serving & frequency capping (only if you consent via the ATT prompt) | No (handled by Google AdMob) |
| **Device & OS info** | iOS version, device model, language, region, screen size | Compatibility, ad sizing | No (handled by Google AdMob) |
| **App interaction events** | Ad impressions, clicks, in-app purchase outcome | Ad measurement, billing | No (handled by Google / Apple) |
| **Crash & performance data** | Stack traces, device state at crash | Diagnosing crashes | Yes, via Apple's crash reporting (anonymous) |
| **Purchase receipt** | Apple StoreKit transaction record | Validating your Remove Ads purchase | No (stored on-device and on Apple's servers) |

### 2.3 Location data

If you enable a location-based feature in the App, iOS will prompt you
for permission. You can grant **"While Using the App"** access, **"Once"**
access, or deny. If granted:

- Location is used **only** to filter or rank in-app reference content.
- Location is processed on your device. It is **not** transmitted to
  DefNotA LLC servers.
- You can revoke location access at any time from the iOS
  Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services screen.

The App does not perform background location collection.

## 3. How Information Is Used

We use the limited information described above to:

1. Display relevant advertising through Google AdMob (when applicable).
2. Measure advertising performance and prevent fraud.
3. Process and validate your optional in-app purchase.

We do **not**:

- Use your information for cross-context behavioral advertising outside

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated **4+** but is intended for general audiences and is not
specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child
under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us at
the address below and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

For users in the U.S., the App complies with the Children's Online
Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by not targeting children and by
configuring Google AdMob to deliver non-personalized ads when the user
indicates they are under 13 (via the ATT prompt or UMP consent flow).

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the
EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, the
California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA / CPRA), or similar U.S. state
laws. These rights may include:

- **Access** — request a copy of personal information about you that we
  hold (note: in most cases we do not hold any).
- **Deletion** — request that we delete personal information about you.
- **Correction** — request that we correct inaccurate information.
- **Opt-out of "sale" / "sharing"** — we do not sell or share personal
  information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
- **Withdraw consent** — for users in the EU/UK, you may revoke
  advertising consent at any time via the App's Settings tab →
  "Manage Ad Consent" option.

To exercise any of these rights, email **defnota.official@gmail.com**. We
may need to verify your request. We will respond within the time period
required by applicable law.

You can also:

- Reset or disable the IDFA in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security →
  Tracking → "Allow Apps to Request to Track".
- Revoke location access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security →
  Location Services → Angler's Ally.
- Delete the App, which removes all on-device data.
